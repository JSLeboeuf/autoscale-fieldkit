Activation Fee: A one-time setup and activation fee is charged at the time of signing. This fee covers onboarding, AI configuration, phone number provisioning, and the 30-day performance guarantee period.
§ 04

Termination & Early Exit

Cancellation After Initial Term. During the month-to-month period following the 12-month initial term, Client may cancel with 30 days' written notice sent to contact@autoscaleai.ca. No early termination fee applies after the initial term.

Early Termination During Initial Term. If Client cancels before the end of the 12-month initial term, an early termination fee equal to 50% of the remaining monthly fees for the balance of the term is due. This fee compensates AutoScale for the dedicated infrastructure and setup costs incurred on the Client's behalf.

Example: A Client on a $697/month plan who cancels after month 4 would owe 50% × 8 remaining months × $697 = $2,788 as an early termination fee.

Termination by AutoScale. AutoScale may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if: (a) Client violates the Acceptable Use Policy (Section 8); (b) Client engages in fraud or misrepresentation; or (c) Client fails to pay fees after receiving proper notice and a 15-day cure period.

Effect of Termination. Upon termination, Client's access to the Service ceases. AutoScale will provide a final export of Client's lead data and call summaries within 30 days of termination. Call recordings are subject to the retention policy in Section 7.

§ 05

Performance Guarantee

Standard Guarantee: If Alex fails to achieve a 90% or greater call answer rate during the first 30 days of active use, Client is entitled to a full refund of the one-time activation fee. Monthly subscription fees already paid are non-refundable under this guarantee.

The 90% call answer rate is measured as the percentage of inbound calls to the Client's AutoScale-provisioned number that are answered by Alex and result in a completed interaction (not dropped, not voicemail-only due to Client-side misconfiguration).

Small Business Guarantee: For businesses receiving fewer than 20 inbound calls per month, AutoScale guarantees a minimum of 5 qualified bookings within the first 60 days of active use, or the activation fee is refunded in full.

To claim a guarantee refund, Client must submit a written request to contact@autoscaleai.ca within 15 days of the guarantee period expiration, including the account identifier and a description of the performance shortfall.

Exclusions. Guarantees do not apply where underperformance results from: Client-side call forwarding misconfiguration, Client-requested custom AI behaviors that reduce answer rates, force majeure events, or periods during which Client's account is suspended due to non-payment.

§ 06

Overage Charges

Each subscription plan includes a defined monthly allotment of AI call minutes. When usage exceeds the included minutes, per-minute overage charges apply at the rate specified in Client's Order Form.

Billing Cycle. Overage charges are calculated at the end of each calendar month and billed in arrears on the following month's invoice date, or charged to the payment method on file.

Notification. AutoScale will send an email alert when Client's monthly usage reaches 80% of the included minute allotment. Clients may request a usage cap to prevent overage charges; doing so will cause calls to go unanswered once the cap is reached.

Overage rates are listed in the signed Order Form and do not change during the initial 12-month term
Unused minutes do not roll over to the following month
Plan upgrades to a higher minute tier can be made at any time and take effect immediately

Data & Call Recordings

Call Recording. AutoScale records calls answered by Alex for quality assurance, AI training, compliance, and dispute resolution purposes. By activating the Service, Client acknowledges this recording practice and agrees to provide appropriate notice to their callers as required by applicable law.

Consent Notice. Alex delivers a consent disclosure at the start of each call (e.g., "This call may be recorded for quality and training purposes"). It is Client's responsibility to ensure this disclosure satisfies the wiretapping and recording laws applicable in their jurisdiction, including two-party consent states.

Data Storage. Call recordings, transcripts, and associated metadata are stored on servers located in the United States (primary) and Canada (backup). AutoScale uses commercially reasonable encryption in transit and at rest.

HIPAA. AutoScale is HIPAA-aware and will execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with healthcare clients upon written request. Standard accounts are not HIPAA-certified by default.

Recordings Retained for 90 days from the date of the call, then permanently deleted.
Transcripts Retained for the duration of the Subscription Term plus 12 months.
Lead Data Retained for the duration of the Subscription Term. Exported to Client upon request.
Billing Records Retained for 7 years as required by applicable tax law.

Clients may request deletion of call data outside these retention schedules by emailing contact@autoscaleai.ca. Requests are processed within 30 days, subject to legal retention obligations.
