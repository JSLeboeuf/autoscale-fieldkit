How to Find Prospects

Step-by-step guide to finding businesses losing calls.

1
Google Maps
Search "[specialty] near me" in your target area. Look for businesses with 50+ reviews (they're busy = they miss calls). Note the business name, phone number, and owner name if visible. Pro tip: Call their number at 6pm. If it goes to voicemail, you have your opener.
2
Yelp
Filter by "Recently Reviewed" — these are active, busy businesses. Check their response time to reviews (slow response = overwhelmed staff). Look at negative reviews mentioning "couldn't get through" or "no one answered".
3
LinkedIn
Search for business owners by title: "Owner", "Founder", "Practice Manager". Filter by your target city and industry. Connect with a short note: "Hey [Name], I work with [specialty] businesses in [City]. Noticed [Business Name] is doing great — quick question about your call volume?"
