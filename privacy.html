§ 01

Overview

9satisfait Inc. d/b/a AutoScale AI ("AutoScale," "we," "us," or "our") operates an AI-powered virtual receptionist platform for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal data in connection with our Service.

This policy applies to: (a) business clients ("Clients") who subscribe to the Service; and (b) individuals who interact with the AI receptionist by calling a Client's AutoScale-provisioned phone number ("Callers").

Plain Language Summary: We collect business contact information, call recordings, and interaction data to operate the Service. We do not sell personal data. Callers are notified of recording at the start of each call. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

AutoScale acts as both a data controller (for data we collect directly from Clients) and a data processor (for Caller data we process on behalf of Clients). Clients are responsible for their own compliance with privacy laws applicable to their use of the Service.

§ 03

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery — Operate the AI receptionist, route calls, book appointments, and deliver call summaries to Clients
AI Model Improvement — Anonymized and aggregated call transcripts may be used to improve conversation quality, reduce errors, and train new AI capabilities. No personally identifiable Caller information is used in model training without explicit consent
Billing & Payments — Process subscription fees, overage charges, and issue invoices
Customer Support — Diagnose technical issues, respond to support requests, and resolve disputes using call recordings as evidence
Performance Reporting — Generate call analytics, booking conversion rates, and usage dashboards for Client accounts
Legal & Compliance — Comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests from authorities, and enforce our Terms of Service
Security — Detect fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse of the platform
Communications — Send transactional emails (receipts, alerts, booking confirmations) and, with consent, product updates and feature announcements
Legal Basis (GDPR): For EU/UK customers, our lawful bases are: performance of a contract (service delivery), legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, AI improvement), compliance with legal obligations, and consent (marketing communications).

§ 05

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

Call Recordings Retained for 90 days from the date of the call, then permanently and irreversibly deleted from all storage systems.
Transcripts Retained for the duration of the active Subscription Term plus 12 months following termination.
Lead & Contact Data Retained for the duration of the active Subscription Term. Exported to Client upon request at termination.
Account & Billing Records Retained for 7 years following account closure, as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.
Usage Logs System and access logs retained for 90 days for security monitoring, then aggregated (PII removed) and retained for 12 months.
Anonymized Analytics Aggregated, de-identified performance data may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

Upon account deletion or subscription termination, all personal data within the retention schedules above is queued for deletion. Deletion is completed within 30 days of the scheduled date, except where legal hold obligations apply.

§ 07

CCPA — California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grant you additional rights.

Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you in the past 12 months
Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (legal obligations, fraud prevention, etc.)
Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold
Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information — though AutoScale does not sell personal data (see Section 8)
Right to Limit: You may limit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information

To exercise CCPA rights, submit a request to contact@autoscaleai.ca with the subject line "CCPA Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days (extendable by 45 additional days with notice). You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf.

Non-Discrimination: We will not deny service, charge different prices, or provide a different level of service to you because you exercised your privacy rights under the CCPA.
§ 08

No Sale of Personal Data

AutoScale does not sell personal data. We do not sell, rent, lease, or exchange personal information about our Clients or their Callers to any third party for monetary consideration or other valuable consideration.

We do not share personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing platforms. The only sharing of personal data that occurs is with sub-processors listed in Section 4, solely for the purpose of operating the Service on behalf of our Clients.

In the event AutoScale is acquired or merges with another company, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction. In such an event, we will notify affected Clients and the acquiring entity will be required to honor this Privacy Policy or provide equivalent protections.

§ 09

Security

AutoScale implements commercially reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit
AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest (recordings, credentials, tokens)
Role-based access controls — employees access only the data necessary for their function
Row-level security policies on all database tables
Regular security reviews and penetration testing
Incident response plan with Client notification within 72 hours of confirmed data breach

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected Clients within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by applicable law.

§ 10

Children's Privacy

The AutoScale Service is designed for use by businesses and their adult clients. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us at contact@autoscaleai.ca.

§ 11
