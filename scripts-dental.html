04 DENTAL CLOSING TIPS
⏰
Best time to visit Tuesday–Thursday, 11am–1pm. Lunch rush = peak missed calls. You're walking in at the exact moment they feel the pain most.
🎯
Decision maker Office manager or lead dentist — NOT the front desk person. The front desk will nod and say they'll pass it along. Find the owner. Ask: "Is the office manager or Dr. [name] available for 60 seconds?"
✨
Magic phrase "How much is a new patient worth to your practice?" — Let them say the number out loud. Once they do, the ROI math does the rest.

Power demo move Ask them to call their own office number while you're standing there. If it goes to voicemail — you just proved the need live, in front of them. No pitch needed. Just silence and a raised eyebrow.
